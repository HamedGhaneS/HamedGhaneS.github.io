About Me

I'm a research scientist with a control engineering foundation and expertise in mathematical modeling, focused on neural interfaces. I build time-series ML systems for EEG/EMG/ECG—from event detection to continuous decoding—and run closed-loop human studies with causal, low-latency inference in Python/LSL.

Currently a Research Associate at the University of Glasgow, I develop BCI systems that combine computational neuroscience with machine learning to create intelligent interfaces between mind and machine.
